I AM TIM BERNERS LEE

I AM THE FOUNDER OF WWW-WORLD WIDE WEB.



HI

Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee OM KBE FRS FREng FRSA FBCS, also known as TimBL, is an English computer scientist best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web. He is a Professorial Fellow of Computer Science at the University of Oxford and a professor at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Wikipedia Born: 8 June 1955 (age 66 years), London, United Kingdom Awards: MacArthur Fellowship, Turing Award, MORE Education: The Queen's College (1973–1976), Emanuel School (1969–1973), Sheen Mount Primary School Spouse: Rosemary Leith (m. 2014), Nancy Carlson (m. 1990–2011) Children: Ben Berners-Lee, Alice Berners-Lee
